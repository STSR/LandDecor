About us

Land Decor Inc. is a Toronto-based landscape design and construction company founded by Jim Ruscica in 1994. Jim and his highly-skilled full-time construction team specialize in outstanding design and quality installation of customized lifestyle concepts that enhance your home and your life.

No two projects are alike, just as no two clients are alike. Each project is approached with the objective of meeting -- but most often surpassing -- the clients’ expectations. Each project is designed to add functionality and value to the property, and of course be pleasing to the eye.

Through our trusted network of suppliers, we can offer our clients the finest materials and we build to the highest quality construction standards. Our landscape designs are less energy intensive and better for the environment. Land Decor delivers a full spectrum of landscape and construction projects, from simple restorations to elaborate lifestyle concepts, always custom designed to meet your specific needs.
